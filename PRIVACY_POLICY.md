# Privacy Policy

## 1. Overview
Harmonize is an open-source statistical data management and analysis platform designed for use by public institutions. Harmonize does not collect, store, or process personal data by default. Any personal or sensitive data processed using Harmonize is determined by the implementing institution.

## 2. Roles and Responsibilities
In deployments where personal data is processed:
- The implementing institution acts as the **Data Controller**
- Harmonize acts as a **Data Processor**, providing technical functionality only

## 3. Personal Data Processing
Harmonize may be configured to process personal or sensitive data (e.g. microdata for statistical purposes) depending on the deployment context. The type of data collected, legal basis for processing, retention period, and access policies are defined by the Data Controller.

## 4. Purpose Limitation
Harmonize processes data solely for statistical production, analysis, and dissemination purposes as configured by the implementing institution, in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

## 5. Data Subject Rights
Harmonize supports compliance with data subject rights under applicable data protection laws, including:
- Right of access
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure
- Right to restriction of processing
- Right to data portability

## 6. Security Measures
Harmonize provides technical and organizational measures to support data protection, including:

- User access control
- Authentication and authorization mechanisms
- Audit logging
- Configurable data governance rules

## 7. International Frameworks
Harmonize is designed to support compliance with international data protection frameworks, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and OECD Privacy Guidelines.
